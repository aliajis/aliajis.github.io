The eighth planet from the sun, Neptune is about the size of Uranus and is known for supersonic strong winds. Neptune is far out and cold. The planet is more than 30 times as far from the sun as Earth.

Name: Neptune
Namesake: Roman God of the Sea
Planet Type: Ice Giant
Moon: 14
Diameter: 49,530 km
Orbit: 165 years
Rotate on its axis: 19 hours
Magnetic field: Roughly 27 times more powerful than Earth's
Distance From Sun:
  • Average: 4,498,252,900 km
  • Closest: 4,459,630,000 km
  • Farthest: 4,536,870,000 km
Temperature: Average: -200 Celcius
Composition:
  • 80% hydrogen
  • 19% helium
  • 1.5% methane
The overall composition of Neptune is, by mass, thought to be about 25 percent rock, 60 to 70 percent ice, and 5 to 15 percent hydrogen and helium
